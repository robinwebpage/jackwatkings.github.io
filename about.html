Meaning, intention, purpose. They’re everything, right?

For brands. For businesses. For all of us.

There’s no denying that a beautiful brand and website will satisfy the eye, but combine that with clarity, strategy, an inspired vision and a willingness to do things differently, and you have the potential to connect to the heart and soul of your dreamiest clients and customers, and compel them to take action.

A £55,000 launch weekend for an online course, a 200% return on investment within a business’ first week of online sales, a product on the shelves of a nationwide retailer, a brand and online store with double the industry average conversion rate – these are just some of the results that I’ve been able to help my clients to create.

Each and every month, there are millions of interactions with brands and websites I’ve helped to bring to life. This is the power of intentional design.

Experience

Before founding my own business, I worked as an in house designer for an international sportswear company, before becoming the Senior Designer for an award winning digital agency.



Working on projects for household names, as well as continued professional development, allows me to stay informed of best practices, industry insights and contemporary techniques across brand, web, design and development, and this is key to my knowledge over assumptions approach.



You may be wondering how I’ve fit all of this into my life so far, and well, I’m probably older than my baby face would have you believe!
